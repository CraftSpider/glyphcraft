## Usage

Part of the chain used when Ezzen modifies Amethyst's mantle to shoot remotely at the oil rig. Also used by Takagiri in her first appearance, remotely transmitting an illusion to observe Ezzen.

## Glyphs

[[{MANIFEST}]] - Creates LM matter, so likely forms the actual item out of LM at the target location

[[{TRANSMIT}]] - Presumably part of the long-distance part, sending something far away from where you are.
