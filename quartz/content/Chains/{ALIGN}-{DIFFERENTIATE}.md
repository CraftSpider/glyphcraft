## Usage

Creates a magical gysroscope cheaply, via multiplexing the four spatial cardinal directions and checking for changes in orientation. Can also tell up, and presumably other directions.

## Glyphs

[[{ALIGN}]] - Presumably tells direction, aligns along axis.

[[{DIFFERENTIATE}]] - Selects things, locates changes in orientation most likely.
