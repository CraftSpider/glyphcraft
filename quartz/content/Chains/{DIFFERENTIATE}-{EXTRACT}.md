## Usage

Cleans a wound by selecting undesired matter and removing it. Similar to [[{DIFFERENTIATE}-{OFFSET}]].

## Glyphs

[[{DIFFERENTIATE}]] - Selects material to remove

[[{EXTRACT}]] - Moves the material out
