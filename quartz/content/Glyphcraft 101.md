Magic in the world of Sunspot is largely based on shards of the Frozen Flame. Flamebearers, humans who bear shards of flame, can wield this flame to perform feats of magic. There are multiple ways to wield magic, but the safest and most well-understood is Glyphcraft, the process of weaving Flame into Glyphs, 2D, 3D, or 4D patterns which have consistent effects and can be chained to perform arbitrarily complex operations.

Not all Glyphs can be chained to all other glyphs. As an example, [[{DIFFERENTIATE}-{SEVER}]] is an invalid combination that results in a Zeno's paradox-like mode of failure. See the linked page for more details.

## Ripple

Ripple is created by both magic and strong flamebearer emotion (possibly also normal human emotion). It appears to behave similarly to a fluid. In a free, uncontrolled form it can be deletrious to human health. It is divided into colors, which are classifications of ripple types that have affinity for certain affects. The colors of ripple can affect the behavior of Glyphs that use it. Glyphs control and limit ripple, using it as part of function, but only the Vaetna can use Glyphs with zero 'waste' ripple. Uncontrolled ripple is called Free Ripple, and can have deletrious effects on the human body in sufficient quantities, in ways likened to radiation.

There are five kinds of ripple that can be used by glyphs: [[Red]], [[Orange]], [[Green]], [[Blue]], and [[Pink]].

Not all ripple can be controlled by glyphs. [[White]] and [[Silver]] ripple both have no _known_ glyphs that can affect them, only manipulable by Flamebearers directly via other forms of magic.

Ripple can be seen via purpose-made tools or sensors, such as Ai's goggles or ripple analysis devices.

## 4D

Along with the arrival of the Flame, came the 4th dimension. It lays alongside normal 3-space, with distance measured in Ana and Kata. Something went wrong in its connection to normal reality[^4.4], so even a small distance away from earth is completely alien and inhospitable. The standard direction for storing things offset into fourspace is ana, though some things (such as Ezzen's spear) are stored kata instead. Some particularly complicated glyphs, 3rd-order ones, make use of the 4th dimension.
